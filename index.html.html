1. Introduction to Zoho Desk

  1. Zoho Desk is a cloud-based help desk platform designed to streamline customer service operations.
  2. End users can raise, track, and respond to support tickets.
  3. It helps ensure timely support through agent workflows and ticket visibility.

2. Logging into the Help Center

  1. Go to your Zoho Desk Help Center URL (e.g., https://yourportal.zohodesk.com).
  2. Click on the 'Login' button in the top-right corner.
  3. Enter your registered email and password.
  4. If you're new, click on 'Sign Up' and register.
  5. Once logged in, you will see the Help Center dashboard.

    3. Submitting a New Ticket

    1. From the dashboard, click on 'Submit a Ticket'.
    2. Choose the appropriate department (e.g., Technical Support, Billing).
    3. Enter a clear subject for your issue.
    4. Describe the issue in detail using the description field.
    5. Set the appropriate priority (High, Medium, Low).
    6. Attach files or screenshots if needed.
    7. Click the 'Submit' button.

      4. Tracking Ticket Status

      1. Click on 'My Tickets' in the Help Center.
      2. You'll see a list of your submitted tickets along with their current status.
      3. Statuses include: Open, In Progress, Resolved, and Closed.
      4. Click any ticket to view full history and responses.

        5. Responding to Tickets

        1. Click on a ticket to open the detailed view.
        2. Scroll to the bottom and type your reply in the response box.
        3. Attach any necessary files to your reply.
        4. Click 'Send' to submit your response to the agent.

          6. Closing Tickets

          1. If your issue is resolved, open the ticket you wish to close.
          2. Click on the 'Close Ticket' button.
          3. Confirm the action if prompted.
          4. You can reopen the ticket later if the issue returns.
